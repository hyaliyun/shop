Modern IP-based TDMA VSAT systems leverage this approach to optimize bandwidth usage and enhance network performance. TDMA has gained popularity in the maritime sector due to its ability to support dynamic bandwidth allocation and improved spectral efficiency. Let me think about scalability for a moment... SCPC systems can be challenging and costly to scale, as each additional user requires a dedicated frequency band. In contrast, TDMA systems are inherently more scalable, as adding more users does not require additional frequency bands; instead, the system dynamically adjusts time slots to accommodate new users.
